JUMP REQUEST FORM

By: Leap Frogs - U.S. Navy Parachute Team
Posted: March 4, 2020


COVID-19 UPDATE

Right now we're limited to the types of events we can jump into. Sorry.

STEP 1: REQUEST A JUMP

Anyone can request us to perform a skydiving demonstration at their event. We skydive into all kinds of events for all types of organizations. The first thing you do to get the skydiving demonstration process started is to complete the online "Jump Request." It's a formal document that let's us know a little bit about your event and organization, and what we'll be jumping into.

Submit your jump request as far in advance as possible, and at a minimum, 90 days before your event. Jump requests received during our show months will be considered on an individual basis, in the event of other cancellations.

Show months

We perform April through December.

STEP 2: JUMP REQUEST REVIEW

When we hit the ground, we'll thoroughly look over your Jump Request. If we can do it, we'll email you a form called, DD2535. It's a formal request for Military Aerial Support.
